Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 6–8 weeks in advance for the best availability, especially during peak season.

Can I customise a package?

Absolutely. Every itinerary can be tailored — just mention your preferences in the contact form above.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is included in the Elite package. It can be added to Explorer and Voyager for an additional fee.

What is the cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before departure. Partial refunds apply within 30 days.
